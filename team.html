MJ

Mrs. Meghana Joshi

President

+91 98500 65522

She holds a B.Sc. in Home Science (specializing in Child Development) and a DTMR certification, with 31 years of experience in the field of disability services. She is a government-certified music therapist. Currently, she serves as the Manager at the 'Sevasadan Dilasa' workshop (catering to adults with intellectual disabilities) and is the President of the 'Kalavaibhav' voluntary organization, where special-needs students undergo physical and mental development through hobby and art activities. Her work involves the rehabilitation of students with disabilities and addressing physical and mental ailments through music therapy. Conducted online music therapy sessions for special needs students; prepared these students for various music therapy competitions; delivered lectures for RCI-accredited CRE courses; and anchored events.
