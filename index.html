Hobby & Arts Activities for Differently-Abled Individuals

In Pune, we help special students grow through dance, music, painting, craftwork, yoga and more — and discover the pure joy of their own creativity.

Why we exist

Our goals

Give the artistic talents of differently-abled individuals room to flourish.

Bring them pure joy through their own creativity.

Encourage physical and mental development through a variety of art forms.

Recognise each person's innate talents and support their rehabilitation.

Beyond the classroom

More than an activity

From metro rides and mall visits to resort camps, festival celebrations and an annual get-together, we create real-world experiences that build confidence and community.

  • A metro-ride experience for students, Pune to Pimpri-Chinchwad.
  • Hands-on shopping at a mall, with parents and members.
  • Festival celebrations — Navratri, Guru Purnima, Ganpati painting.
  • An annual get-together (Snehsammelan).
