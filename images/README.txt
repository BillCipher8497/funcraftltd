IMAGES FOLDER — Kalavaibhav Sevabhavi Sanstha
=============================================

GALLERY PHOTOS (required)
  Drop your photos here named exactly:
      gallery1.jpg, gallery2.jpg, ... gallery50.jpg
  - .jpg extension, lowercase, no spaces.
  - They are auto-shuffled and placed across the Home/About pages,
    and shown (all 50, randomized) on the Gallery page.
  - Fewer than 50 is fine: any missing file is hidden automatically,
    so the layout never breaks. To use MORE than 50, change
    TOTAL_IMAGES at the top of ../main.js.

LOGO (optional but recommended)
  Save the rainbow-tree logo as:
      logo.png
  It will appear automatically in the header and (if you wire it)
  the footer. If absent, the text logo is used instead.

DONATION QR (optional)
  Save as donate-qr.png, then uncomment the QR block in contact.html.
