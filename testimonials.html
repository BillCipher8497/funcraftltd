Parents' voices

What families tell us

GP

Gayatri & Vikrant Purkar

A parent's reflection

Kalavaibhav Sevabhavi Sanstha is an organization that serves as a "Kamadhenu" (a source of fulfillment) not only for special-needs children but also for their parents. Words fall short when describing the organization's experienced directors and teachers. Through the initiatives undertaken here, the parents — along with the children — experience holistic development. Indoor and outdoor activities allow us parents to discover the latent talents within our children. All these activities are well-planned and executed. The various vocational-oriented initiatives successfully implemented by the organization for special-needs children within a very short span of time pave the way for their self-reliance; the credit for this goes entirely to the Kalavaibhav organization. It is my sincere wish that the sapling planted by the organization's directors and teachers grows into a mighty banyan tree, spreading out in many branches.

SK

Sandhya & Neha Kulkarni

A parent's reflection

Neha Kulkarni has been attending the "Kala Vaibhav Sevabhavi Sanstha" for the past three years, and she is always happy and excited to come here. Thanks to the immense patience, affection, and guidance of the teachers, she has undergone an astonishingly positive transformation. The welcoming and supportive atmosphere here has brought a new sense of confidence to her face. Caring for children with disabilities is a noble calling — a form of dedicated service. Driven by boundless patience, an outpouring of immense affection, and unwavering faith in each child's potential, the teachers here strive to make them self-reliant.
